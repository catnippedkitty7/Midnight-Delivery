How To Play

Scroll for more ⌄
👆
Move with your thumbs
Touch the left side of the screen to move left. Touch the right side to move right.
🥞
Collect the pancakes
Drive through pancake pickups to fill the order card at the top of the screen.
📍
Make the delivery
Once the order says READY, drive into the Lighthouse Cafe delivery zone.
✕
Build your multiplier
Each successful delivery raises your multiplier for future points. The multiplier does not retroactively change old points.
🚗
Avoid traffic
Traffic is coming toward you. One crash ends the shift unless you have a Syrup Shield ready.
💨
Near misses
Skim close to traffic for bonus points, but do not chase near misses if you are boxed in.
🛡️
Syrup Shield
Blocks one crash and clears nearby traffic. It is rare, so treat it like insurance.
🌀
Calm Zone
Slows the action down so you can escape traffic or line up a clean delivery.
☕
Espresso Boost
Gives a short 2x scoring rush and faster driving. It is best when you already have a delivery or a safe lane ahead.

First Run Tips

  • Start by staying near the center lane. It gives you more room to react.
  • Your first goal is simple: collect pancakes, then deliver them.
  • Do not worry about near misses until you understand the traffic rhythm.
  • Power-ups are helpful, but survival and deliveries matter most.
  • Use the Garage to unlock and customize cars as you earn achievements.
